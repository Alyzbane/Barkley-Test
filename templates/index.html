Barkley: Tree Bark Image Classification

Nestled within the serene surroundings of the First Asia Institute of Technology and Humanities Colleges, our project sheds light on the distinctive barks of five indigenous species prevalent in this locale. These encompass the majestic Roystonea regia, the resilient Cananga odorata, the lush Mangifera indica, the vibrant Tabebuia, and the sturdy Pterocarpus indicus.
